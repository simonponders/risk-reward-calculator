// Risk Assessment Framework

FOUR DIMENSIONS
OF CRYPTO RISK

01
💧

Liquidity Risk

Depth and stability of available liquidity. A pool that looks deep today can drain overnight in adverse conditions.

  • TVL vs. daily volume ratio
  • LP concentration risk
  • Withdrawal queue depth
  • Slippage at trade sizes
  • Protocol reserve ratio
02
🏦

Seniorage Risk

Token emission schedules, inflation mechanics, and treasury dilution that silently erode value over time.

  • Annual token emission rate
  • Vesting schedule exposure
  • Protocol Controlled Value
  • Governance mint/burn control
  • Token price vs. intrinsic value
03
⚡

MEV Attack Risk

Maximal Extractable Value exposure — sandwich attacks, front-running, and back-running that silently extract from your trades.

  • Private mempool / Flashbots protection
  • TWAP vs. spot oracle usage
  • Transaction ordering guarantees
  • Slippage tolerance config
  • Historical MEV extraction data
04
📉

Dilution Risk

Future supply expansion reducing per-token value. FDV vs. circulating cap discrepancies are a common trap.

  • FDV vs. circulating market cap
  • Upcoming unlock schedules
  • DAO supply governance proposals
  • Liquidity mining duration
  • Treasury token sell pressure
